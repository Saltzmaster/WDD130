Overview

Purpose

If you were going to introduce the planet to someone who's never been here before how would you do it? How would you describe each country without bias and incorrect information? Well that's what I'm here to do but for my fictional world of ____ from the book I'm writing. The front page will be an interactive map of the world! Then each country will have their own page designed from the perspective of a delegation from their own country making it as fair and accurate as possible. There will also be a smaller map on each individual page showing their countries boarders. This'll make it as interesting as possible while being easy to understand. Doesn't this make you curious to just see the map?

Audience

It'll be targeted towards teens to young adults. Mostly the nerdy kids who've read a lot and who read fictional books.
