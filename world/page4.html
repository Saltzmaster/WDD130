The Orhil

The Orhil are definitely the apex. They are considered the most beautiful race and their agility and connection to the Nothing are top notch. They look like a human snake hybrid. They two arms and to legs but they have scales instead of skin and their neck is fanned while their head is triangle shaped. They have a long tail and golden eyes. Their appendages all work like that of a human as well as the added functions a snake would have. Their lean muscles and slick but tough skin combined with their ability to perfectly grasp tools and weapons allows them to reign supreme on this world.

The Orhil's system

Due to their high connection to the Nothing the Orhil do not need anything special to help them cast spells. Instead they can draw them in the air. The only limits from them are skill and imagination. It’s often thought that their spellcasting is the slowest but widely agreed one that it’s the strongest and most flexible of the mage arts. The strongest mages have been known to cast spells almost instantly with the smallest hand and finger movements possible sense the size of the runes is irrelevant to the power of the spell.
